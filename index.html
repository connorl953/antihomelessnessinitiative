Our Story

The Sentinel Anti-Homelessness Initiative began as the COVID-19 pandemic shed light on a dire food security issue amongst this communityâ€™s most vulnerable and at risk communities. Their unfortunate circumstances imposes difficulties to access regular filling meals. The Sentinel Anti-Homelessness initiative seeks to aid those in need through fundraisers, charities, and food donations through sponsorships. Monthly meetings and workshops are held to discuss the direction of our initiative and craft/produce food items to be donated and distributed.
