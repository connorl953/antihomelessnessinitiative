Core Values

Sentinel Anti-Homelessness initiative is a youth-led non profit organization spanning across multiple schools. Monthly meetings will be held to plan, craft, and distribute care packages and food items.

The Plan

Recruitment

Conceptualize and finalize The Sentinel Anti-Homelessness Initiative, establish a school club and begin with recruitment for members and volunteers.

Expansion

Discussions are being held with other schools in hopes of expanding the Anti-Homelessness Initiative and as a result, increasing our impact on the community and becoming a multi-school organization.

Workshops/Distribution

Workshops will be held close to weekends monthly where members will plan, and craft care packages and food items. Members will sign up to meet on either the saturday or Sunday of the same week to distribute these products to those in need.

Sponsorships

Local organizations and corporations will be restlessly contacted by our fundraising team for sponsorships or generous donations to help us further aid those in need.

Food

Monthly meetings and workshops are held to discuss the direction of our initiative and craft/produce food items to be donated and distributed. Hygiene will be our number one priority when constructing the food items and care packages, with the founders and seniors to ensure the quality of the food is up to the organization’s standards.

Our Story

The Sentinel Anti-Homelessness Initiative began as the COVID-19 pandemic shed light on a dire food security issue amongst this community’s most vulnerable and at risk communities. Their unfortunate circumstances imposes difficulties to access regular filling meals. The Sentinel Anti-Homelessness initiative seeks to aid those in need through fundraisers, charities, and food donations through sponsorships. Monthly meetings and workshops are held to discuss the direction of our initiative and craft/produce food items to be donated and distributed.
